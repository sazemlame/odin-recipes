Stamppot: A Dutch Classic

Stamppot is a Dutch classic mostly consumed during winter.

Ingredients

  • 500gm potatoes
  • 150gm borenkool
  • 200gm sliced onions and carrots
  • 100gm butter
  • Salt and Pepper as per taste
  • 100ml of milk/fresh cream
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft
  2. Cook the carrots and onions in butter until soft in a deep pot
  3. Add the potatoes after peeling to the carrots and onion hustpot
  4. Smash the potatoes and add milk/fresh cream slowly until malleable
  5. Add Borenkool to the pot and mix
  6. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
